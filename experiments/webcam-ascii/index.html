Webcam ASCII

Your camera feed, translated into characters in real time. Every frame the video is sampled down to a grid, each cell's luminance mapped to a position in the ASCII ramp — dense characters for dark areas, sparse for bright.

Switch to color mode and each character inherits the actual RGB of its source pixel. Nothing is stored or transmitted — the entire pipeline runs locally in your browser using getUserMedia and Canvas 2D.
