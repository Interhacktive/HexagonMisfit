Text Flow

Real-time text reflow around floating physics bubbles, powered by pretext — a pure-arithmetic text measurement engine that calculates line breaks without touching the DOM.

Bubbles drift and collide with buoyant physics. Click one to pop it — particles burst outward and a synthesized sound plays. Click empty space to spawn new bubbles. Text reflows every frame as shapes move, using pretext's variable-width line layout.
