Webcam Physics Blob

An interactive physics sandbox that uses your webcam to detect dark objects and convert them into physics boundaries. Draw a rectangle to define the play area, place dark objects inside, and watch balls interact with the detected shapes in real-time.

The system processes your webcam feed in real-time, applying thresholding to convert the image to black and white, then uses blob detection to find dark objects and convert them into vector shapes that act as fixed physics bodies.

// interaction

Draw Boundary Click and drag to draw a rectangle that defines the physics boundary area.
Drop Balls Click anywhere above the boundary to drop physics balls that will interact with detected objects.
Threshold Slider Adjust the brightness threshold for detecting dark objects. Lower values detect darker objects.
Ball Size Control the size of the physics balls that are dropped into the simulation.
Gravity Adjust the gravity strength in the physics simulation.
Clear Shapes Remove all detected physics shapes from the simulation.
Reset All Clear all shapes and balls, returning to the initial state.
