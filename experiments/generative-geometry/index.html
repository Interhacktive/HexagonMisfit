Generative Geometry

A triangulated spring mesh that breathes with simulated audio and reacts to microphone input in real time. Every vertex is physically simulated — spring force, damping, and velocity all computed per frame on the CPU.

Bass frequencies pulse the center outward. Mids ripple through the middle ring. High frequencies shimmer across the edges. Click or tap the canvas to fracture the mesh and watch it reassemble through spring physics.
