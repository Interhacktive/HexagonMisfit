The Project

An indoor interactive LED sculpture featuring a star-shaped form with individually addressable elements. Waves of light flow through the structure in mesmerizing patterns, perfectly synchronized to an audio track that plays through integrated speakers in the base.

The installation is designed for simple deployment — just plug it in and the experience begins. The synchronized light and sound create an immersive, meditative environment suitable for lobbies, galleries, and event spaces.

Technologies

  • Custom LED fabrication
  • Arduino
  • Audio synchronization
  • Plug-and-play installation
